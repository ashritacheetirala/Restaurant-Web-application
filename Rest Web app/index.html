About Us

Forge is a playful cantina with menus that reflect both the vibrancy of Mexico's cuisine and the Midwestern seasonal harvest. We have sought to curate an atmosphere that acts as an extension of the guest's dining table at home and invites them to take a journey through Mexicoâ€”paying homage to culinary traditions of specific regions while providing a modern, Oaxacan-inspired perspective. The significance behind the concept's name comes from a flying ant, 'Forge,' that is native to Mexico. The celebrated insect tells a story of springtime migration during the rainy season, symbolic of progress despite otherwise rough conditions. Our goal is to embody the resilient Forge spirit, flowing with the seasons and the landscape of Chicago's hospitality industry.
